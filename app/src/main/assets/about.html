Health & Eco Commitment

Children, Pet, and Environment-Safe Extermination Benchmarks

At Jay Vijay Pest Control, your health is our supreme priority. Conventional pest control relies on highly volatile, hazardous compounds that leave toxic residues on floorboards, furniture, and toy surfaces. We completely reject these unsafe methods.

We work exclusively with certified low-toxicity biological chemicals, odorless herbal gels, and species-specific gel baits. These compounds target pests' specific metabolic and neurological pathways, remaining completely harmless and non-reactive to humans, dogs, cats, and indoor ornamental plants.
