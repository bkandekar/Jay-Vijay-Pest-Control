Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2026. Learn how we safeguard your personal and property privacy.

1. Introduction

Jay Vijay Pest Control ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website jayvijaypestcontrol.github.io. We are committed to protecting your physical privacy as well as your digital confidentiality. We treat all personal and business data with the highest standards of safety, ethics, and regulatory compliance.

2. Zero Server-Side Storage Policy

Unlike conventional web services, we do not compile, collect, save, or store any personal information on web servers or external online databases. Our digital booking forms are completely frontend-driven and execute purely within your local browser sandbox.

When you fill out our digital booking or contact forms, the text values are instantly processed locally on your client device using JavaScript. The code creates a pre-formatted, URL-encoded string payload. It then initiates an outbound browser redirect to WhatsApp's secure API servers to complete the message generation, keeping your entries away from server exposures.

3. WhatsApp-Only Communication Flow

By using our interactive booking form, you acknowledge and agree that your booking parameters will be transmitted through end-to-end encrypted WhatsApp channels directly to our active office desks (+91 7559352915 / +91 9922464685).

Any communication files, property photos, address details, or schedule variations shared via WhatsApp are stored inside our offline business systems. They are used strictly to coordinate service dispatching and invoice generation, and are never shared with any marketing networks.

4. Cookie & Local Tracking Policy

We do not deploy advanced user tracking or dynamic tracking scripts. We utilize basic, standard technical session cookies and local storage parameters only for functional purposes:

  • To remember if you have dismissed the top promo banner to prevent repetitive interruptions.
  • To maintain navigation states as you browse across the different page modules.
  • To track general, anonymous site performance telemetry via basic static hosting metrics.

5. Your Rights & Data Access

Since your digital bookings reside only in your personal WhatsApp chat history and our local business registry, you retain complete authority over your information. At any point, you may request our customer care staff to erase your offline booking record, contact logs, or home address directories from our active dispatch lists.

6. Updates to This Policy

We reserve the right to revise this Privacy Policy periodically. Any modifications will be updated on this page with a revised effective timestamp. We recommend reviewing this document occasionally to stay updated on our data security policies.

7. Help & Support

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, our zero-storage setup, or our WhatsApp-based client workflows, feel free to contact us at jayvijaypestcontrol@gmail.com, call our offices at Vijapur Road, Solapur, or chat with our team directly on WhatsApp.
