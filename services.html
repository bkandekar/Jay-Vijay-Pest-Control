Full Service Catalog

Professional Biological & Chemical Control

Odorless, safe, and highly targeted treatments targeting every major pest group found in Solapur residences and commercial spaces.

General Pest Control

Broad-spectrum eradication targeting overlapping crawling insects, centipedes, common ants, silverfish, and wall spiders in domestic environments.

Standard Procedure: Perimeter synthetic barriers, liquid sprays on floorboards, crack dusting.

Termite Control & Inspection

Advanced detection of subterranean mud tube pathways, wood injections, soil termiticide pumping, and structural barrier seals with a 5-year guarantee.

Standard Procedure: Sub-tile Drill-Fill-Seal chemical barrier injection + soil saturation barriers.

Cockroach Extermination

Eradication of German and American cockroaches from warm nesting zones like kitchen drawers, appliances, and drain lines using odorless bio-gels.

Standard Procedure: Dots of odorless biological gel baiting + chemical growth regulators.

Rodent / Rat Control

Strategic trapping and baiting of bandicoots, sewer rats, and mice. Involves installing lockable external station grids loaded with single-dose rodenticides.

Standard Procedure: Weatherproof bait boxes + physical glue traps on active runways.

Bed Bug Extermination

Aggressive eradication of blood-sucking bed bugs hiding in bed borders, seams, baseboards, and couch seams using dual contact chemical sprays.

Standard Procedure: Deep structural chemical spraying scheduled twice over a 15-day period.

Mosquito Control & Fogging

Control of disease-carrying mosquitoes via thermal diesel smoke fogging, liquid larvicide drops on standing water, and residual mist barriers.

Standard Procedure: Outdoor smoke thermal fogging + indoor ultra-low volume (ULV) misting.

Ant Extermination

Complete colony eradication of sweet ants, black garden ants, and destructive carpenter ants nesting inside wood crevices and hollow doors.

Standard Procedure: Systemic baiting syrups (ants carry biocide to queen) + chemical dusting.

Bee, Wasp & Hornet Removal

Safe, specialized physical extraction of dangerous insect hives and honeycombs hanging from balconies, high building beams, or vents.

Standard Procedure: Late evening knock-down sprays under thermal suits + physical colony removal.

Spider Extermination

Deterring cobwebs and structural spider infestations on high ceilings, structural beams, crawlways, and outdoor balconies.

Standard Procedure: Web clearance sweeps + residual perimeter repelling chemical sprays.

Bat Extermination

Humane extraction, repelling, and sealing of wild bats occupying roof valleys, high false ceilings, and unused warehouse lofts.

Standard Procedure: Non-toxic physical ultrasonic repellents + entry-hole netting barriers.

Flea & Mite Extermination

Eradication of micro-fleas, bird lice, and skin mites nesting inside animal bedding, thick carpets, and attic vents.

Standard Procedure: Fine contact chemical fog misting + sanitization treatments.

Home / Property Pest Inspection

Thorough visual review of foundation cracks, pipeline entry seals, crawl spaces, and roofing joints to detect pest nesting sites early.

Standard Procedure: Thermal sensor search + colony mappings + inspection review report.
